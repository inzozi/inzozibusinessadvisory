Services Offered

We are committed to providing high-quality, personalized, and timely service to our clients. We offer a wide range of services to meet your accounting and tax needs. Here are some of the services we provide and how they can benefit you:

  • Business registration and licensing

    We offer assistance with registering and licensing your business in Rwanda. This includes selecting the appropriate legal form, name, and structure, completing necessary paperwork, and obtaining required permits, licenses, and certificates from relevant authorities.

  • Accounting and bookkeeping

    We can handle all your accounting and bookkeeping tasks, such recording transactions, preparing financial statements, reconciling accounts, and maintaining ledgers. We can also help you set up and manage your accounting system, whether it is manual or computerized.

  • Tax compliance and advisory

    We can help you comply with the tax laws and regulations in Rwanda and internationally. We can prepare and file your tax returns, calculate and pay your tax liabilities, and claim your tax refunds. We can also advise you on tax planning and optimization strategies, such as tax incentives, exemptions and deductions

  • Financial management and reporting

    We provide support in managing and reporting your financial information, including preparing and analyzing budgets, cash flows, forecasts, and projections. We also help monitor financial performance, identify risks, and resolve any issues.

  • Payroll Processing

    Ensure accurate payment of employee salaries and benefits with our efficient payroll processing services. We handle everything from tax compliance to filing payroll taxes

  • Financial Data Analysis

    Make data-driven decisions with our advanced financial data analysis services. Our team will collect, organize, and analyze your financial data to provide actionable insights and recommendations.
